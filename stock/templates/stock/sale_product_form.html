Link

A product can either link to a RECIPE (the bill of materials) or to another PRODUCT (e.g. Pack/6 → Loose). Either choice carries a quantity + unit, so "Focaccia 3.75kg" links to its recipe at 3.75 weight_kg, and "Pack/6" links to the Loose product at 6 count.

Examples: 1 + units = a simple loose product; 6 + units = Pack/6; 3.75 + kilograms = a 3.75 kg slab.
